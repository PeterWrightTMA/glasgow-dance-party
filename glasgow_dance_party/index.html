What is Glasgow Dance Party?

Glasgow Dance Party is a dance party in Glasgow designed and organised by Glasgow's top amateur dancers. No skill + loads of enthusiasm = Glasgow Dance Party. We want to run the best outside dance party since records began, and we need YOU to do it.
